How to Find Your NYT Cookie

To access subscriber-only recipes, you need to provide your NYT-S cookie:

  1. Open cooking.nytimes.com in your browser
  2. Log in to your NYT Cooking account
  3. Press F12 to open Developer Tools
  4. Click on the Application tab (Chrome) or Storage tab (Firefox)
  5. In the left sidebar, expand Cookies
  6. Click on https://cooking.nytimes.com
  7. Find the cookie named NYT-S
  8. Double-click the value and copy it
  9. Paste it in the "NYT Cookie" field
